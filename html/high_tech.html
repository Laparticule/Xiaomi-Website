Drones

Mi Drone 4K      

4K | Flight time 27 minutes | Distance control 2000m

Xiaomi has created an amazing technology which is so simple that even a novice will able to control the Mi Drone UAV via its application. Now you can generate routes, set up automatic takeoffs and landings in order to fully immerse yourself in the enchanting sceneries. The Mi Drone will give you a fresh look at the world with the easiest operation.

Main Features:

  • 4K UHD camera supports up to 3840 x 2160P 30fps video recording
  • Automatic takeoff and landing
  • Setting up a destination
  • Automatic flying around a given point
  • Route planning
  • Automatic return
  • 27 minutes of continuous flight

Erida Drone      

4K | Flight time 40 minutes | 7Mph

The YI Erida is exceptionally fast and agile, reaching speeds of up to 75 miles per hour (120 km/h) and flying for up to 40 minutes in test flights. The full carbon fiber body and unique three-rotor design is not only extremely lightweight, but also highly durable.

Main Features:

  • This breakthrough drone also features the YI 4K Action Camera with up to 30fps video recording.
  • YI Erida features an adaptable and customizable design. The drone is built ready to fly, but will be adaptable for further modifications.
  • Full carbon fiber body. 5 times stronger than steel, although it weight 2/3 less.
