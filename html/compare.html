Comparing Xiaomi and Apple

Is the comparison relevant?

If Xiaomi has been considered China's 'Apple' due to its resemblance with the famous brand (a resemblance that has been criticised a lot) it appears that the company is now capable of creating innovative products, maybe even better than Apple's. Better products, at a much lower price, who wouldn't dream of that? The following is a specification comparison between the two flagship products of the brands: the Iphone X and the Mi Mix 2.
